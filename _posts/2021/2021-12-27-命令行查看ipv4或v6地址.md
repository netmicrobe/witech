## icanhazip.com

~~~
curl icanhazip.com
# 或
curl ipv6.icanhazip.com
# 或
curl --ipv6 icanhazip.com

# 查看v4地址，如果是双栈
curl ipv4.icanhazip.com
# 或
curl --ipv4 icanhazip.com

# 制定网络适配器查看
curl ipv4.icanhazip.com --interface wlp39s0f3
~~~
